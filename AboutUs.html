About Us

Who are we?

We are a group of 5 students who have been commissioned to create a website about sustainable programming. Together, we work to make the site as user-friendly as possible, with good content that can help everyone from students and hobby programmers to professional developers. All of us ar taking classes at University of South Eastern Norway on the subject Practical Project Management on the information processing study at USN, we have different backgrounds in education and profession.


Our Mission

The unique purpose or objective of the project is to develop a website or platform that educates and inspires software developers, students, and organizations to adopt energy-efficient, eco-friendly, and socially responsible coding practices. The project is based on the concept of new software development as it aims for creating an entirely new software application from scratch.

The project is temporary as the timeline is from January to April 2025. The project drives change and enables value creation as it is an educational and development-focused initiative centered on promoting sustainable programming practices.

The project team consists of five students at University of Southeast Norway meeting consistently throughout the timeframe of the project.
