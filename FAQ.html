Question

What branches of IT and technology development can benefit from using green programming tools and frameworks?

Answer

All IT development projects can benefit from using green programming, but some types of project have a larger potential for saving power and resources. Power use in servers and other hardware is growing and optimization of the hardware usage can be a great place to start. You can read more about this in our articles.
